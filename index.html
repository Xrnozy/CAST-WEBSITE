About Us

The Collective Arts of Students & Thespians (CAST), founded in March 2005 at the University of Makati, is a cultural organization that fosters collaboration among student-artists and thespians from various disciplines. CAST is committed to using art to raise awareness and inspire social change, focusing on performances that resonate with youth and address relevant societal issues.

Our Mission

The Center for Culture and the Arts envisions a vibrant and relevant set of activities by the various and diverse affiliate student-organizations and the whole UMak community, and its business partners that promote the culture and the arts while espousing excellence with a soul and competence with compassion.

Our Vision

To promote cultural excellence and arts appreciation, UMAK – CCA initiates creative and multi-faceted artistic performances that advocate the ideals of the true Great Brave Herons, the Makatizens, the Filipinos, and all citizens of humanity.

Our Objective

The Center for Culture and the Arts is a creative and artistic department that harnesses the potentials not only of its student – members but also of UMak’s stakeholders: employees, alumni, parents and industry partners. This goal is achieved through dynamic and significant curricular and co-curricular programs that enhance artistic self-expression, specifically in the discipline of performing arts.

Core Values

We have an unwavering commitment to:

Truth

Brotherhood

Excellence

Service

The CAST upholds Truth (Katotohanan), Brotherhood (Kapatiran), Excellence (Kahusayan)
and Service (Paglilingkod) as their Four Core Values.
